Features included

  • Want to rename your todo title? Just edit the title by pressing enter or clicking refresh icon and save it
  • Adding the task is as easy as typing the text. Just press enter or click on enter icon and save it in the list below. Task with no description are not allowed
  • Had a little typo while adding the task? No worries, just click on the item you want to edit. It will hightlight the task as well. Once you are done just hit enter
  • Want to prioritize your to-do? Use three-dots icon or simply hold the task and drop it wherever you want
  • Done with the task and want to mark it as a complete? Simply click on checkbox to mark it as complete. It will strike of the item from the list
  • Want to remove the clutter from your todo? Simply click on clear completed task after marking it complete
  • Want your todo list back same as you left? Don't worry we will save every action on your browser because we know its important for you :)
